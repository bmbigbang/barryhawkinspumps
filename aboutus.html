About Us

Our principles in doing business includes giving you a friendly costumer service, with the most efficient solution as well as arrangement for your convenience and effective maintenance.

We can provide support for almost all Stuart Turner pumps, and our prices come directly from them as we are their servicing representitive. We can arrange for part replacements as well as repairing old parts and existing faults, and finally full servicing of your current pump.


Over the last decade we have increased our clients and worked to ensure their satisfaction with the pumps in use. The range of products is rather diverse and includes everything from small shower pumps to larger commercial pumps.
